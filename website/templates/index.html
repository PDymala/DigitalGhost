Patterns in random images

From more than a million images, only 15 were picked.

Usually when we think about computers, algorithms, maths, we assume that those are flawless. Because how could a computer make a mistake in adding two numbers? That can only be because of human error. Although when talking about new age programming, those cannot exist without machine learning and that is a whole other topic. During this project, I made an experiment aimed at that particular branch of science

I programmed a software that automatically generated random images of a given size and tested it in search of face detection. As the feature detection algorithms are relatively slow, it took a couple of days to generate and check more than a million images. For the same reason face detection was chosen as it's one of the fastest models available.

Machine learning is known for being not 100% accurate. Was this a mistake or is there something that a human eye cannot see?
